The idea

PictoricBox is a collection of 150 unique non-generative artworks. It is a creation from sixteen talented artists that have brought something unique to the table.

We believe that art has the power to unite people. That's why we've put that as an idea behind PictoricBox. It represents the results of one year of tight collaboration between different artists working in their unique styles.

At first, it was released as a board game and received warm feedback. Now you can own one of the illustrations as non-fungible tokens. Each NFT allows its owner to obtain a physical copy of a board game.

Our vision is to allow token owners to have an exclusive take on building their version of game rules and contribute to the metaverse.

Follow us on social media to be up to date with our latest news and announcements.
